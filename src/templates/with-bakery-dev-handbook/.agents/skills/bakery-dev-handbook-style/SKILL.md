Read and follow the documents below before writing or editing Luau scripts.

- `./references/bakery-dev-handbook/docs/frameworks/scripting/scripting-guide.md`
- `./references/bakery-dev-handbook/docs/frameworks/scripting/structure-style.md`

- `./references/bakery-dev-handbook/docs/frameworks/scripting/syntax-style.md`
- `./references/bakery-dev-handbook/docs/frameworks/scripting/naming-style.md`

- `./references/bakery-dev-handbook/docs/frameworks/scripting/document-based-comment.md`

Do not invent conventions that are not written in the guides.
Do not add undocumented keywords or syntax (for example, moonwave `@module` tag).
